My Approach to AI

I believe there are three main skills for mastering AI:

  • Modeling: Knowing the math and methods behind statistical modeling, machine learning, and deep learning.
  • Researching: Staying curious and focused, especially in a your niche, to innovate and add depth to AI projects.
  • Scaling: Applying software engineering skills to make sure AI solutions work reliably in real-world settings.

Mastering these skills helps create AI solutions that are understandable, reliable, and ready for real-world challenges.
